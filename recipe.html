Garam-Masala


Ingredients: Coriander Seeds; Cumin Seeds; Cardamom,
Cloves, Fennel Seeds; Dry Red
Chili; Black Pepper Corns; Bay Leaf; Cinnamon; Black Cardamom; Salt, Star Anise,
Mace

Recipe: Prepare the vegetable of your choice with usual spices
in ghee/oil as you normally do. Add 1/2 tsp Garam Masala.
Stir vegetable on a low flame for 3 mins. Keep covered for atleast 5 mins.,
so that the vegetable retains the flavour and aroma of Garam Masala (500 gms.
vegetables or 1 litre of ready dal needs 1 and 1/2 tsp Garam Masala).
Samosa, potato vada, kachori and all types of
farsans taste best with Garam Masala.
TIP: 500 gms vegetables or 1 litre of ready dal needs 1½ tsp Garam Masala.

Sunday Masala



Ingredients: (Mutter Paneer) in 1 tbsp oil light fry 1 chopped onion, 2 green chillies,
2 cloves of garlic, 5 cashewnuts, 1 tbsp poppy seeds,
1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp kasuri methi, 1inch ginger.
Add 1/2 cup water, keep for 30 min, grind to fine paste.
Fry Paste in 3 tbsp ghee for 3 min.
Add 2 purred tomatoes, 1 tsp chilli powder,
1/2 tbsp fusion turmeric powder & 2 tbsp Sunday Masala.
Add 1 cup boiled peas. Cook till ghee seprates.
Add 1/2 cup water & 250gms fried panner.
Cook for 5 mins.

Black Pepper Corn, Dry Mango Powder,
lodized Salt, Kashmiri Red Chilli,
Cinnamon, Cloves, Fennel Seeds, Green Cardamom

Recipe: In 2 tbsp oil, shallow fry 2 tsp garlic paste and chopped onions till soft.
Add tomatoes, Pav Bhaji Masala, Chilli Powder and salt to taste.
Add boiled vegetables. Mash and cook for 2-3 min.
Add a blob of butter and sprinkle chopped coriander leaves.

Biryani Masala


Ingredients: Coriander Seeds; Turmeric;
Cumin Seeds; Black Cardamom; Green
Cardamom; Mace; Mustard Seeds;
Fennel Seeds; Black Paper; Clove; Nutmeg;
Cinnamon; Star Anise; Poppy Seeds;
Dry Ginger; Bay Leaves; Sun Dried Red Chili;

RECIPE:Before you begin: Parboil 250 gms. Basmati Rice.
In 3 tbsp oil, fry 3 sliced onions, 1 tsp each ginger/garlic paste.
Add puree of 2 tomatoes, chilly and turmeric powder.
Cook till oil separates. Add ½ cup curd.
Add 1/2 tbsp Biryani Masala, roast.
Add 1½ cup cooked vegetables like green-peas, cauliflower, carrot and salt.
Grease bowl, spread the rice, add vegetables, spread remaining rice.
Pour 2 tbsp ghee, ½ cup milk. Cover, heat for 15 mins. on low flame.

Black Gram, Coriander seeds, Turmeric,
Cumin, Red Chillies, Cloves, Mace, lodised Salt,
Fenugreek Seeds, Mustard, Black Pepper, Cardamom

Recipe: For the marination, take a bowl and mix chilli powder,
coriander powder, turmeric powder,
curd and salt together, Now, wash the chicken in cold running water and
chop it. Once it is done, add the chicken pieces to the marinade.
Keep them aside for 4-5 hours.heat oil in a pan.
Once the oil is sufficiently hot,

Chicken Masala


Ingredients: Coriander Seeds, Red Chilli, Fennel Seeds, lodized Salt,
Turmeric, Cinnamon, Fenugreek Seeds,
Mustard Seeds, Black Pepper, Cloves, Nutmeg,
Green Cardamom, Cumin Seeds, Mace, Bay Leaves

RECIPE: MEAT Before you begin: To cook 500 gms. of Mutton.
Grind to fine paste 2 onions. Mix with 1 tsp Ginger/Garlic paste.
Add salt and 4 tbsp strained curd.
Marinate cleaned and cut mutton pieces with mixture.
Keep for 2 hrs. Heat 4 tbsp oil, fry till crisp 2 sliced onions.
Add marinated mutton, 3 tsp Mutton Masala. Stir for 5 mins.
Add 2 cups water. Pressure cook for 10 mins.

Fish Curry Masala


Ingredients: Red Chili, Coriander Seeds,
Cloves, Mustard Seeds, Turmeric Powder,
Mace, Cumin Seeds, Poppy Seeds,
Fennel Seeds; Bay Leaf; Stone Flower; Fenugreek,

Recipe: To cook 750 gms. of fish, cut into 1/2 inch cubes.
Rub the pieces with lemon juice and salt keep aside for 30 min.
Gently fry to golden colour, drain and keep warm.
Saute 2 chopped onions for 10 min, drain oil and add 1/2 cup yogurt.
Blend it and stir for 3 min on
medium heat adding 1 tsp ginger garlic paste.
When brown, add 2 chopped tomatoes, 2 tsp Fish Curry Masala,
1/2 tsp Chili powder, 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder stirring till thickened.
Add fish, cover, simmer for 2 min and
garnish with 2 tsp fresh coriander leaves.

Fish Fry Masala


Ingredients: Asafoetida, Chilli, Coriander, Cumin Seeds, Curry Leaves,
Dry Ginger, Fennel Powder, Garlic, Tamarind, Turmeric

Recipe: Fish Fry Masala Before you begin: With
a sharp knife make fine slits on pieces of Fish.
Apply lime and salt. Keep for 15 mins.
Mix 4 tsp ginger/garlic paste, 4 tsp Fusion Fish Fry Masala,
6 tbsp strained (thick) curd,
salt and food-grade orange red colour.Marinate cut Fish with mixture.
Keep aside for atleast 2 hrs.
Apply 2 tsp melted butter on the marinated Fish and roast till well done.
This masala can also be used for other Fish Fry preparations.
